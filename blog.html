Tacloban City marks 129th Sto. Niño fiesta
  • By Jane Brown
  • In Community
  • 2 Comments

Hundreds of devotees of Sto. Niño trooped to the Sto. Niño Church on June 30 for the 129th Sto. Niño de Tacloban fiesta.

Naval Bishop Emeritus Filomeno G. Bactol celebrated a Pontifical fiesta Mass at 9:30 a.m. concelebrated by bishops from Leyte and Samar.

More than 100 priests from the Archdiocese of Palo and the suffragan dioceses of Eastern Visayas also concelebrated with Bactol, himself former parish priest of Sto. Niño.

Sto Niño Shrine in Tacloban undergoing repairs
  • By Jane Brown
  • In Community

TACLOBAN CITY — One of the sequestered properties of the Marcoses here has been undergoing some facelift.

At least P10 million was set aside for the repair of the Santo Niño Shrine and Heritage Museum, a sprawling property built by former first lady Imelda Marcos from 1979 to 1981.
